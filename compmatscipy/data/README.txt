This README describes the files in comp-mat-sci-utils/datasets


Gels.json
-dictionary of elemental chemical potentials from experimental data at integral temperatures from 300-1800 K and 1 atm
-associated with https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-018-06682-4


masses.json
-dictionary of atomic masses for the elements
-associated with https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-018-06682-4


mus.json
-dictionary of DFT-calculated elemental chemical potentials
-associated with https://arxiv.org/abs/1810.08248 (npj DOI coming soon)

tau_prob_clf.p
-pickled probabilistic perovskite predictor based on tau 
-associated with https://arxiv.org/abs/1801.07700 (Sci Adv DOI coming soon)

shannon_radii.json
-dictionary of Shannon revised effective ionic radii
